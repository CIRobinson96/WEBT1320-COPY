What is All Elite Wrestling?

All Elite Wrestling is a professional wrestling company founded in 2019 by President and CEO Tony Kahn. In its brief, two year history AEW has risen to be the #2 wrestling promotion in the world. AEW boasts one of the most talented rosters in professional wrestling history, each of them competing week in and week out for one of AEW's 4 prizes: The Championships.
